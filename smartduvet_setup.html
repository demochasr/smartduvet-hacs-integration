SmartDuvet Wi-Fi Setup

Use this page on your phone while near the SmartDuvet. Everything happens locally via forms in a hidden iframe (no CORS headaches, no new tabs).

👀API Response Viewer
1 Connect to the SmartDuvet hotspot
SSID: SmartDuvet
Password: smduvet@2011

Tip: scan the QR to auto-fill Wi-Fi on most phones.

2 Scan & View Device Info

You can skip this if you already know your home Wi-Fi SSID/password.

After scanning, open info to see nearby 2.4 GHz SSIDs listed under wifi_available.

3 Send Home Wi-Fi Credentials

This stores SSID/password on the device. It won’t connect yet.

4 Trigger Connection

After 2–10 seconds, the device will try to join your home Wi-Fi.

5 Status: Watch for sta_ip

When connected, sta_ip in the viewer above will show the device’s LAN IP (e.g. 10.0.0.23). That’s the IP you’ll enter in the Home Assistant integration UI.
